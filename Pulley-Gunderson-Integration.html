Announcing our new integration with Gunderson

August 30, 2023

By Yin Wu, Co-Founder & CEO

We're thrilled to announce a new integration with Gunderson Dettmer. If you're launching and incorporating a startup through Gunderson, you can now instantly onboard to Pulley’s equity management platform.

How startups benefit

Pulley was built on the idea that equity is your startup's most valuable asset. Starting with an accurate, up-to-date cap table isn't just housekeeping. It's a strategic move that streamlines fundraising and hiring.

Joining Pulley through our integration with Gunderson gets your cap table up and running right away. After onboarding to Pulley, you also get access to essential tools for fundraising, hiring, 409A valuations, and more.

How it works

With the launch of this integration, getting organized is easier than ever. Instead of a manual, days-long onboarding, your legal team can push data directly into Pulley. Cap table information automatically pre-populates, awaiting final approval. This saves valuable time and streamlines the onboarding process for everyone involved.

This collaboration marks the first time two organizations are using the Open Cap Table Coalition’s open-source data standard. Gunderson is a founding member serving on the steering committee, and Pulley has been a member of the coalition since 2021.

A shared commitment

Pulley is proud to be partnering with Gunderson on this integration. Gunderson has demonstrated a commitment to enabling a technology-forward workforce and leveraging best-in-class tools to service its startup clients. Since 2020, Pulley and Gunderson have helped hundreds of shared clients manage their equity and scale their companies.
